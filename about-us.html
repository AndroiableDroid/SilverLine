Our Story

At Silverline Inc, our journey started with a simple belief — great design has the power to transform a space. What began as a passion for crafting high-quality aluminium tables has grown into a full range of stylish, durable furniture and home decor items that help people turn their visions into reality.

As a manufacturer and exporter of aluminium coffee, console, and side tables, along with other home decor pieces, we’ve always been driven by a commitment to craftsmanship and attention to detail. Every piece we create is built to last, with designs that are both timeless and modern.

But we don’t just stop at offering ready-made products. We’re here to bring your ideas to life. Whether you have a specific design in mind or need something completely unique, we specialize in product development tailored to your exact needs.

At Silverline Inc, we’re not just making furniture — we’re helping you make your space feel truly yours.

Our Mission

At Silverline Inc, our mission is to provide high-quality, beautifully crafted aluminium furniture and home decor that enhances the spaces our customers live and work in. We are committed to delivering exceptional products with an unwavering focus on durability, design, and customer satisfaction. Whether through our ready-made collections or custom product development, we aim to offer timeless pieces that inspire and elevate any environment.
